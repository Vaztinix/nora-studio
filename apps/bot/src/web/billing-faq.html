Premium Billing FAQ

Frequently asked questions about managing your Nora Premium subscription, billing operations, and settings.

How do I purchase a Nora Premium subscription?

Nora uses Discord's official **App Subscriptions** system. To upgrade:
  1. Open the server where you want to apply premium.
  2. Go to the **Nora bot profile** (click on Nora in the member list or user popout).
  3. Click the **Upgrade** or **View Premium** button on the profile.
  4. Complete the checkout flow powered securely by Discord.

How do I check if my server has Premium activated?

You can easily check your premium entitlement status by opening the **Nora Studio Dashboard**, selecting your server, and looking at the **Nora Premium** section at the top of any configurations page. If active, you will see a badge indicating **PREMIUM ACTIVE**. You can also run the `/premium` slash command in your server to view details.

How do I manage, change, or cancel my subscription?

All payments and recurring subscriptions are handled securely through the Discord client:
  1. Open your Discord app and click the **Settings Gear** next to your avatar.
  2. In the left-hand sidebar, select **Subscriptions** under the **Billing Settings** section.
  3. Locate your **Nora Studio** subscription card.
  4. Click **Manage** to upgrade, downgrade, edit payment methods, or click **Cancel Subscription** to stop recurring billing.

Can I transfer my Premium subscription to another server?

Yes, if you purchased a user-level premium tier, your entitlements follow you! You can assign your premium boost slot to any server you own or manage. Simply open the **Nora Dashboard**, head to **Personal Settings**, select your active subscription details, and use the **Transfer Slot** dropdown to bind it to a new server.

What happens to my settings if my Premium expires?

If your premium status expires, your configuration data is not deleted immediately. However, premium-locked features (such as advanced logging, regex custom filtering, live presence tracking, or large-volume application configurations) will be disabled until a premium slot is re-activated or provisioned.

Who do I contact for billing issues or refund requests?

Because all billing and transaction processing is handled exclusively by Discord, Nora developers do not have direct access to your credit card data, bank information, or transaction records. For any refund requests, billing errors, or subscription invoice issues, please contact **Discord Support** directly at **https://support.discord.com/**. For non-billing technical issues, join our support server!
