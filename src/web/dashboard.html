Managing Subscription & Cancellations

Premium upgrades and subscriptions for Nora Studio are processed and handled directly through the Discord client using App Subscriptions. If you need to modify, upgrade, or cancel your active subscription, please follow these steps:

  1. Open your Discord client and navigate to User Settings (the gear icon next to your avatar).
  2. Under the Billing Settings section on the left sidebar, click on Subscriptions.
  3. Find your Nora Studio subscription card and click on Manage or Cancel Subscription.
  4. Follow the prompts to finalize the cancellation or configuration of your plan.
