Frequently Asked Questions

How fast do Roblox verification updates sync?

For standard users, Roblox ranks and profile connections verify instantly via our custom validation codes. Rank synchronizations are executed on periodic checks. Premium guilds enjoy direct instant rank bindings.

Why isn't my custom autoresponder firing?

Check if the command card has "Ignore Server Staff & Bots" toggled on, or if you have configured allowed/ignored role filters that exclude your profile. Administrators can verify active rules in the dashboard settings panel.

Can I delete all my stored community data?

Yes. In the interest of total data sovereignty and privacy, users can execute the `/mycard` command in Discord at any time and click the "Erase Data" button to instantly wipe their warnings, leveling stats, and verification mappings.
